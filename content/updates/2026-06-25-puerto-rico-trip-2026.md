We landed in Puerto Rico on the 21st of June 2026. We had been planning this Puerto Rico trip for months! When we arrived at our Airbnb we decided to go get food. Once we finished our food we returned home and went to bed.

---

**June 22nd, 2026** — We woke up, went to the grocery store, came back home, then went to the beach. Overall chill day.

---

**June 23rd, 2026** — We went to a rainforest — an hour long hike — then got food and went to the beach for a little bit. Went back home.

---

**June 24th, 2026** — We went to two beaches in the morning and afternoon. Had a great time. We are a little sunburnt. 😎
